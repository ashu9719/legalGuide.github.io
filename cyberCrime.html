Think Before You Share

Our Mission

To create a safer digital environment by educating individuals and organizations about:
  • Recognizing potential cyber threats.
  • Implementing cybersecurity best practices.
  • Building a culture of vigilance and informed decision-making.

What We Offer

  1. Educational Campaigns:
    • Interactive workshops tailored for schools, businesses, and communities.
    • Awareness initiatives that promote actionable tips to prevent cybercrime.
  2. Cyber Hygiene Programs:
    • Guidance on application security, updating software regularly, and securing personal data.
    • Training on identifying phishing attempts, ransomware risks, and social engineering tactics
  3. Support for Victims:
    • Assistance in understanding what to do if targeted by cybercriminals
    • Collaboration in reporting incident and providing Justice though legal channels

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual is equipped with the knowledge to navigate the digital landscape safely. By partnering with governments, schools, businesses, and NGOs globally, we aim to reduce cybercrime incidents significantly.
